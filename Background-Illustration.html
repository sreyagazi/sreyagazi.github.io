Background Illustration

10x10x24 Paper Stop-Motion Backgrounds


↑ Paper stopmotion is such a useful tool for creating lots of animation with a 2D-style visual appeal in a very fast-paced way which was perfect for the 10x10 (1 animation everyday for 10 days) project. These were different backgrounds I drew and coloured with colouring pencils in my sketchbook with the intention of animating on top of them using paper stopmotion.
Paper stopmotion essentially involves making stopmotion animation using flat paper backgrounds and cutouts of drawings.

Aphrodite and Artemis / Divine Feminine Project


↑ This is a paper stopmotion project I was able to conceptualise and produce in its entirety in the space of one afternoon. I used cutouts of printed statues, small drawings and dialogue written on little bits of paper and a background illustration in my sketchbook drawn using a marker and some colouring pencils.
