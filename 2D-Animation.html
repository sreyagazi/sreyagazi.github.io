2D Animation

Wither (10x10x24)

↑ This animation was one of my favourite personal entries to the 10x10x24 challenge, a challenge which involved having to create and submit one finished animation project every single day for ten consecutive days in 2024.
It features two bunny rabbits planting seeds and dancing for rain after a forest fire. The narrative is based on the following lyrics from the song 'Wither' by Frank Ocean: "Over where the trees burned down / The place where the fields went down in flames / We could put a whole in the ground / Throw seeds and dance for rain."
This was animated digitally using Procreate Dreams.

Traditional Animation

The following two examples of my animation work were made by drawing each frame manually on paper with the help of a lightbox. The images of each frame were then cut together, like stop-motion, using the program DragonFrame. ↑ The first real works of handdrawn animation I ever made. The ↑ video contains 3 animations: one of a bouncing ball, one of a diver diving into a pool and the final is a creative work of a girl opening an enchanted box.
I created all of these works in 2021 at a Summer Animation short course for teenagers held at UAL Central Saint Martins. ↑ Animated traditionally as a group project, responding to the prompt 'Shiny, shiny' during induction week at Edinburgh College of Art (2023). My partner animated the first half (blue ink) and I animated the second half (black ink).
